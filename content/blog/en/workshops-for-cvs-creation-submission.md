In May 2022, Nideport carried out the first job search, focusing on the project's area of influence and oriented to the local community. Nideport's Community Relations area received the CVs and provided advice on the production and delivery of CVs.

CVs were received in two ways:

By mail to: (eugeniastrobbe@gbmcoin.io)

Reception at Nideport's office in Cruce Caballero.

For those who did not know how to prepare a CV, during the week of May 15th to 22nd, 2022, Nideport's Community Relations Department, in addition to receiving CVs, held a CV preparation workshop every day from 9 a.m. to 12 p.m., so that applicants could prepare their CVs in situ.
